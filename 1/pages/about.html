About USF Malawi

Who We Are

USF is a specialized unit under MACRA, established under the Communications Act (Cap 68:01) to promote universal access to ICT services across Malawi.

Our Mission

  • Promote universal access to broadcast, postal and telecommunications services
  • Bridge the digital divide between urban and rural communities
  • Ensure digital inclusion for all Malawians
